Auto-send email (EmailJS + your Gmail)

A web page can't log in to Gmail directly, so this uses EmailJS to bridge the connection. Each company sets up its own EmailJS account so emails send from their own Gmail:
1. Free account at emailjs.com.
2. Add an Email Service → Gmail → connect your Gmail. Copy the Service ID.
3. Create a Template with variables to_email, client_name, doc_type, doc_number, total, business_name, and an attachment field pdf_attachment. Copy the Template ID.
4. Account → General → copy your Public Key.
